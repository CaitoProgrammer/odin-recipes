How to make a Matecito

There's nothing more flavourous than un buen mate argentino

Ingredients

  • Bombilla
  • Mate
  • Hot water (not boiled)
  • Some herbs
  1. Put the Bombilla within the Mate
  2. Put herbs inside
  3. Pour the hot water until reach top of herbs

Thats everything now enjoy the best drink all over the world.
