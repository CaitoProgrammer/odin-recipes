CheeseBurger

Hot to make a Cheese Burger

This is a good meal if you want to invite yours friends. This time we'll do 4 burgers, if you want to make more... just double quantities.

ingredients

  • Burger bread (4 units)
  • Minced meat (at least half a kilogram)
  • Cheese
    • Parmesian cheese (100gr)
    • Cheddar cheese (100gr)
  • Onions (1 per two portion)
  • Pickle (1 big unit)
  • Tomato (2 or 3 units)
  • Buttered Lettuce
  • Some eggs
  • Seasoning
    • Something spicy is the best option
    • Salt
  • Some dressing or sauces
  • Lettuce

Also you'll need the rights tools when cooking

You'll need:

  • An spatula
  • A cooking griddle
  • A cutting board
  • A bowl

And of course the obvius cutlery...

Steps

  1. Make sure you're preheating your griddle
  2. Put the minced meat, the eggs, and condiments in the bowl and mix
  3. Cut the vegetables the way you want
  4. Cut and toast the bread, i recommend use some butter
  5. Give the meat some sphere form and smash it

  6. Put the meat on the iron until is golden and well cooked.
    When golden turn around and add the cheese until is melt.

  7. Prepare the sauces, i'd like to do a Tasty Sauce (Ketchup, mayoinase and cigulette)
  8. Take every preparation to the table, i'd like to invite the diners to prepare their own burger the way they want.
